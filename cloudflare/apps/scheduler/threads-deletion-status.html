Data deletion — nothing stored on our servers

Drew's Video + Socials Scheduler is a self-hosted application: your Threads access token and all related data live only on the computer where you run it (in your operating system's keychain and a local SQLite database). There is no server operated by us that stores your data, so there is nothing for us to delete.

To fully remove the app's access: revoke it in your Threads / Meta account settings, then on your own machine delete the app's data directory (~/Library/Application Support/com.nuclearcyborg.drews-socialmedia-scheduler/ on macOS) and the matching keychain entries (search Keychain Access for com.nuclearcyborg.drews-socialmedia-scheduler).
